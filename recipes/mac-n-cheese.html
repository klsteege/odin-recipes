Dad's Mac & Cheese

Description

This mac & cheese has been a part of my life from the beginning. It is creamy and gooey with just the right amount of stringiness to the cheese.

    Ingredients

  • 8 oz macaroni - the shape is up to you!
  • 8 oz cheddar cheese
  • 1 Tbs butter
  • 1-2 Tbs flour
  • 1 can tuna
  • 1 cup whole milk
