Edamame Bean Salad

Description

This salad was found online. It is delicious, and can be flavored to your preference. Great side or meal for spring & summer. We usually forget about it during the colder months, and then are pleasantly surprised when we remember it again.

    Ingredients

  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 5 Tbs red wine vinegar
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 3 Tbs grapeseed oil
  • 1/3 cup cilantro, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 lb pack frozen shelled edamame
  • 3 cups frozen corn kernels
  • 1 or 2 red peppers, chopped
  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 can black beans, drained & rinsed
  • 2 4oz cans green chilies
